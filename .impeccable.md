## Design Context

### Users
People looking for a private, appointment-based wellness/optimization practice — the kind of patient who chooses their doctor the way they choose a tailor. They read carefully, value the long arc, and are drawn to practices that feel considered rather than corporate. The clinic serves as a single-practitioner operation (Dr. Vin) offering cannabis medicine, peptide therapy, and longevity/optimization work in a Bondi Beach setting.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **considered, coastal, unhurried.**

Unhurried apothecary. Salt-air meets a quietly authoritative clinician. The voice is intelligent and slightly aloof — like Aesop's product copy, not like a wellness influencer. Zero hype. Zero "book now and unlock your potential" language. Editorial confidence.

### Aesthetic Direction
Bondi-Beach apothecary. Sun-bleached limestone and ocean glass meet a printed medical pamphlet from 1974. Flat blocks of color for a geometric horizon (sun, sky, sea) — never gradients. Editorial serif display paired with a crisp modern grotesk. Vertical sidebar wordmark, numbered service blocks, a scrolling tagline marquee. Wide margins, varied spacing, deliberate asymmetry.

**References**: Aesop store signage; Bondi Icebergs pool; The Byron at Byron hotel collateral; 1970s Swiss pharmacy leaflets; Parley for the Oceans editorial.

**Anti-references**: glassmorphism wellness landing pages; "modern clinic" templates with teal gradients and rounded cards; biohacker dashboards with neon accents; anything with a stock-photo palm tree.

**Theme**: Light — warm limestone cream, NOT sterile hospital white. A twilight/ocean block appears only as a flat horizon decoration.

### Design Principles
1. **Editorial over medical.** Read like a magazine, not a clinic brochure.
2. **Flat geometry over depth effects.** No glassmorphism, no drop shadows as decoration, no gradient text. Horizons are flat color bands with a hard edge.
3. **Rhythm through asymmetry.** Varied column widths, numbered sections, a vertical sidebar, marquee moments. Never a grid of identical cards.
4. **One moment that stays with you.** The sun-over-horizon hero + the rotating vertical wordmark are the hooks.
5. **Coastal without cliché.** No palm trees. No "surf's up." No seagull silhouettes. The sea shows up as a color and a horizon line, nothing else.
