02This website is not medical advice

The content on this website is general in nature and is provided for informational purposes only. It does not constitute medical advice, a diagnosis, or a therapeutic claim. Nothing on this website should be relied upon as a substitute for a consultation with a registered medical practitioner. Treatment decisions are made during a clinical consultation after a proper assessment of your individual circumstances.

03Telehealth consultations

All consultations are conducted by phone. Video consultations are not offered. You are responsible for ensuring you are available at the scheduled time, in a private location, and able to answer your phone when called. Telehealth is not appropriate for all clinical situations. If Dr Patel determines that your concern requires in-person examination, imaging, or specialist assessment, you will be referred accordingly. You must be physically located in Australia at the time of your consultation.

04Prescribing

Any medication prescribed is based on clinical assessment and is at Dr Patel’s sole clinical discretion. A booking does not guarantee a prescription. Prescriptions are issued only where clinically appropriate after a proper medical assessment, and within the Australian regulatory frameworks administered by the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA). Any additional regulatory requirements that apply to a particular therapy are explained during the consultation. You are responsible for informing Dr Patel of all current medications, supplements, and relevant medical history. Incomplete or inaccurate disclosure may affect the safety and appropriateness of any treatment.

05Bookings and appointments

Appointments are booked through our practice management platform (Tacklit). You will receive confirmation and any required intake forms by email. First consultations are approximately sixty minutes. Follow-up consultations are shorter and scheduled based on clinical need. You are expected to attend your appointment at the scheduled time. See our Cancellation and Refund Policy for details on cancellations and late attendance.

06Fees and payment

Consultation fees are displayed at the time of booking and are charged via Stripe through our practice management platform. Payment is taken at the time of booking or prior to the consultation as specified. Some consultations may attract a Medicare rebate where eligibility criteria are met. This is discussed at booking. The cost of any prescribed medication is separate and is paid directly to your pharmacy.

You agree to provide accurate and complete information during intake and consultations. You agree to attend scheduled follow-up appointments where these form part of your treatment plan. You agree not to use this service for emergency or urgent medical care. For emergencies, dial 000. For after-hours non-emergency concerns, call Healthdirect on 1800 022 222.
